about

Who I Am

I learn cybersecurity by breaking systems to understand how to secure them. Pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science at Mahindra University, I'm building a strong foundation with a focus on real-world threat analysis and system-level risk.

My approach is hands-on and curiosity-driven — I actively explore how vulnerabilities emerge, how they can be exploited, and more importantly, how they can be mitigated. Through practical labs I've worked on password security analysis, network reconnaissance with Nmap, traffic behavior with Wireshark, and designing a CVSS-based vulnerability management system.

What drives me isn't just the tools — it's the mindset. Thinking in terms of systems, identifying weak points, and understanding how attackers and defenders approach the same problem differently.
